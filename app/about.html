About F&G Re Ltd

F&G Re Ltd is a wholly owned subsidiary of FGL Holdings domiciled in Bermuda. Its affiliates include F&G Life company of Iowa and F&G Life of New York. These companies have provided retirement and protection solutions for over 40 years. As the newest member of the F&G family, F&G Re works to extend this expertise, dedication to excellence, and innovative customer solutions beyond the shores of the United States.
