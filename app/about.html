About F&G Re Ltd

F&G Re Ltd is a wholly owned subsidiary of FGL Holdings domiciled in Bermuda. Its affiliates include Fidelity & Guaranty Life Insurance Company(FGLIC) and Fidelity & Guaranty Life Insurance Company of New York (FGLICNY). These companies have provided retirement and protection solutions for over 40 years. As the newest member of the F&G Holdings family, F&G Re works to extend this expertise, dedication to excellence, and innovative customer solutions beyond the shores of the United States.
